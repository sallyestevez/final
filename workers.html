Amazon is Known to Mistreat Their Workers

The median annual pay for Amazon workers is $28,446.

Most of Amazon's emplyees work in fulfillment centers.

The median annual pay at Facebook is $240,430.

Amazon uses an app to track and discipline delivery drivers.

Amazon requires contracted delivery drivers to download an app, called Mentor, that tracks and scores their driving behavior.

Even though the app is supposed to improve driver safety, Amazon employees say that the technology produces errors and can even track their location after they're done working.
